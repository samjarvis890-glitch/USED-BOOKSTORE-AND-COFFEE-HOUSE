Getting Here

Transit & Parking Directions

Driving & Parking

Free parking is available in the rear lot behind the bookstore. Additional street parking is available along Lover's Lane.

Public Transit

Take Bus Route 14 or 22 and exit at the Coffee Haven Plaza station. We are located a short 3-minute walk north.

Accessibility

Our entrance is equipped with automatic doors. Ramp access is available at both the front and rear entries.

Coffee Haven

Our Location

Find Us in the Heart of the City

Located right next to the Historic Central Library, Pages & Perks is easily accessible via public transit and features customer vehicle parking at the rear. Look for the vintage brick facade and the irresistible aroma of freshly roasted coffee.

Public Transit

3 minutes walk from Central Station (Line B). Take the South Exit and head down Literary Way.

Parking Options

Free backyard parking with purchase validation. Enter via Maple Alley behind the store.
