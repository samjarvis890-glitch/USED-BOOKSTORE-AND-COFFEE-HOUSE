Timeline of Milestones

How a simple bookshelf evolved into a premium coffee house sanctuary.

2012 — The Genesis

Tiny Bookstore Corner

Clara Vance opened a small corner bookstore with only 1,000 used books, operating in a historic brick house.

2017 — The Cafe Fusion

Specialty Coffee Integration

Partnered with master barista Julian Reed, installing a premium espresso machine and converting half the shop.

2023 — The Lounge Expansion

Renovating The Lounge

Relaunched the loyalty ranks, expanded our floor plan to 5,000 square feet, and introduced daily literary readings.

Our Calling

To recycle stories, elevate sips, and spark conversations.

We believe a bookstore is more than a retail shop; it is a community sanctuary. By circulating pre-loved books, we preserve literature sustainably, while our hand-poured coffees create space for quiet contemplation and warm friendship.

Recycling literary works reduces waste and extends the carbon cycle. Every traded book is a green step forward.

Community

Conscious Culture

Caring for Pages & Planet

At Pages & Perks, we believe that the stories of tomorrow depend on how we treat the earth today. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact through sustainable sourcing and community partnerships.

  • 100% biodegradable, plant-based cups and straws
  • Organic, fair-trade coffee beans sourced directly from family farms
  • Book circular economy - recycling and donating unsellable volumes

Our Community Pledge

We donate 5% of all coffee sales on the first Tuesday of every month to local public libraries and child literacy programs.
